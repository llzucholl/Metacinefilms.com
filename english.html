ABOUT US

In METACINE FILMS we take care of creating attractive and high quality audiovisual products. We are situated in the Colombian southwest and a big part of our projects have been developed in the country side, making team with farmers. We have the capacity, creativity and commitment in order to work so that many stories can be told. We love new technologies, through them we have also presence in Mexico, USA and Chile. We offer the multimedia and transmedia tools so that with art and professionalism, the concepts and ideas of our clients can be expressed into visual experiences and reach the eyes and ears of its target public.

TEAM

We know how important and accurate the audiovisual is for transmit information nowadays, that’s why we are integrated as a multidisciplinary team that produce innovative, dynamic and quality contents. We try to constantly exceed the expectations of creativity and functionality thanks to a continuous communication with the client to build as a team a high quality product. We always explore the best strategies that catch and seduce publics with increasingly demanding visual expectations. With knowledge of our profession we make every effort to achieve successful projects that achieve the communicative intent of our customers.
